About Us

Gada Electronics, led by Mr. Jethalal Champaklal Gada, is a leading electronics retail company in Mumbai. The company offers a wide range of innovative products to meet diverse customer needs. Under Gada's leadership, the store has expanded its product portfolio and gained a reputation for excellent customer service. The store's knowledgeable staff assists customers in making informed decisions, ensuring they find the perfect electronic solutions. Gada Electronics also offers online shopping through a user-friendly website, demonstrating its commitment to adapting to modern consumer needs. The company remains committed to integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction, looking forward to serving the community for many more years.
